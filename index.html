Hello! I’m Jonas Neubert. My research interests are romantic relationships (particularly non-heteronormative ones) and serotonergic psychedelics. I have interviewed couples about their psychedelic experiences (link to the pre-print) and hope to do more qualitative research in the future. I have recently completed my research internship at the Neuropsychology and Psychopharmacology research department at Maastricht University and my master’s thesis titled “Relationships and psychedelics: Does a shared LSD experience alter relationship quality and conflict behaviour?”
